What Is Computer Programming?

Computer programming is the process of developing instructions for a computer to execute. Famous tech companies like Google, Facebook, Twitter, Microsoft and Apple, couldn't exist today without the development of computer programming. Developing basic computer programs is possible on any computer at your disposal. Computer code resembles human language, however, it follows specific syntatic rules due to the nature of computers themselves. The possiblities of what can be programmed is theoretically infinite and is only limited the technology of hardware and software.
